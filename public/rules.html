Imarika Rules and Guidelines

Last updated: July 6, 2025

1. Bundles Rules

  • Bundles are subject to availability and may change without prior notice.
  • Only users who meet the eligibility criteria may redeem bundles.
  • Bundles are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash.

2. Eligibility Rules

  • You must be a Kenyan resident aged 18 years or older.
  • Provide a valid Safaricom number and pass identity verification checks.
  • Eligibility decisions are made automatically and are final.

3. Referral Rules

  • Referrals must be genuine and not self-referrals.
  • Referral rewards are issued only when the referred user completes eligibility.
  • Fraudulent activity may lead to disqualification.

4. Promotion Rules

  • Promotions are time-bound and may change without notice.
  • Each user can only participate once per promotion.
  • Promotions are governed by Imarika's internal criteria.

5. Offers Rules

  • Offers are limited and issued on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Offers cannot be combined with other discounts unless stated.
  • Unused offers expire after a set period.

6. Account Deletion Rules

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Deleting your account will forfeit any pending rewards or bundles.
  • User data will be removed in line with our privacy policy.

7. Tracking Rules

  • Progress tracking is automatic and updated regularly.
  • Delays in status updates may occur based on verification time.
  • Users are responsible for ensuring their activities meet tracking requirements.

8. Privacy Rules

  • User data is handled per the Imarika privacy policy.
  • No personal data is shared with third parties without consent.
  • Users may request access or deletion of their data at any time.
